About Us:

The Dixon Event Center is the main venue for the worlds largest sporting and music events and fashion shows! In the past we have hosted events like SuperBowl, Rolling Loud and the Met Gala. A unique featue of this venue is that we allow clients to host their own personal events if needed like a weddings, receptions, birthdays, or babyshowers!
